Page Colourizer Options

You can include random patterns from ColourLovers.com. Patterns are applied to the background of the page, with the pattern's colors being applied to the page just like palettes. Including patterns can make pages look pretty garish.

Maximum color ENGAGE! With Colour Frenzy mode, every page you load will be colorized automatically. Not for the faint of heart.
